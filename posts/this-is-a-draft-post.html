This is a Draft Post

This post is marked as a draft in the frontmatter with draft: true.

What this means:

  • ✅ The HTML file will be generated at posts/this-is-a-draft-post.html
  • ✅ You can view it directly by visiting that URL
  • ❌ It won't appear in the posts list page
  • ❌ It won't appear on the homepage recent posts
  • ❌ It won't be included in pagination
  • ❌ It won't be included in category filtering

How to publish:

To publish this post, simply remove the draft: true line from the frontmatter or change it to draft: false.

Draft indicator:

When viewing a draft post directly, you'll notice:
- The title has a [DRAFT] prefix
- The post is generated but hidden from public listings

This allows you to:
- Work on posts in progress
- Share draft links for review
- Test content before publishing
